Teriyaki Salmon

Home

Description

Teriyaki salmon is my husband's favorite dish. This recipe never fails to be a hit, whether we broil the salmon in the oven or grill it outdoors. Pair it with some homemade teriyaki fried rice to complete your meal.

Ingredients

  • Sesame Oil: This teriyaki marinade for salmon starts with sesame oil.
  • Lemon Juice: Lemon juice adds welcome freshness and brightness.
  • Soy Sauce: Soy sauce lends tons of savory flavor.
  • Brown Sugar: Gives the marinade pleasant sweetness.
  • Sesame Seeds: A tablespoon will add mild flavor and crunch.
  • Spices: You'll need ground mustard, ground ginger, and garlic powder.
  • Salmon: This recipe makes four salmon steaks.

Directions

  1. Make the marinade on the stove, then set aside 1/4 cup for basting.
  2. Marinate the salmon in zip-lock bags in the fridge for up to two hours.
  3. Broil the salmon until it flakes easily, brushing with the reserved marinade as-needed.
